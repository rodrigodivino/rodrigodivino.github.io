VAST Challenge 2019 - Disaster in St. Himark!

The VAST Challenge (Visual Analytics Science and Technology Challenge) is an annual data analysis challenge. The VAST Challenge 2019 was hosted at IEEE VIS conference in Vancouver.

In 2019, the challenge was to help a fictional city cope with an earthquake. The inhabitants have applications in which they can report the level of destruction, and the decision makers of the city want to analyze this data in real time to direct efforts to the right plaes, even in the face of uncertain data

My solution to this challenge was awarded the title    "Award for Supporting Situation Awareness and Anomaly Detection" The submission consists of a customized dashboard for monitoring the data in real time:

The Dashboard contains four views connected by interactions. It is possible to filter data over time or by measurements, which allows analysts to answer the critical questions that the situation requires.

The submission accompanied a video, in which I describe each view and the interconnected interactions.
